Module 18
D1/GPIO5 - SerBrTx (71) - connect to Drok pin T
D2/GPIO4 - SerVrRx (72) - connect to Drok pin R

G pin connect to Drok G pin

SerialConfig 3 (8N1)
SerialDelimiter 13
